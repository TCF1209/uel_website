Choosing The Right Oil

Synthetic, semi or mineral?

  • Fully synthetic oils are often chosen for modern engines, higher heat stability and longer performance reserve.
  • Semi-synthetic oils can be a practical balance for many daily-use vehicles and workshops.
  • Mineral oils may suit selected older engines, basic service needs or industrial applications when the correct specification is used.
Malaysia Use Case

Heat, traffic and heavy work.

  • City driving creates repeated stop-start heat cycles, so oil cleanliness and oxidation resistance matter.
  • Diesel fleets and delivery vehicles need attention to load, hours, drain interval and oil top-up habits.
  • Factories should match hydraulic oil to pump type, operating temperature, contamination control and machine manual.

Safe recommendation: check your owner's manual or machine manual first. When asking UEL, share vehicle or machine model, year, current oil grade, usage style, quantity needed and delivery area.
